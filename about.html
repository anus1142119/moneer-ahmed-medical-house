Medical Philosophy & Expertise

Dr. Ahmed's approach to medicine is rooted in the Bio-Psycho-Social model, recognizing that optimal health outcomes require attention to biological factors, psychological well-being, and social determinants of health. His practice emphasizes:

  • Evidence-based clinical decision making
  • Patient-centered care with emphasis on health literacy
  • Preventive medicine and lifestyle interventions
  • Adherence to Royal College clinical standards
